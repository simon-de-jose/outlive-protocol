# Target Ranges (Generic Attia/Outlive Framework)

User-specific targets, baselines, and progression are in the baseline file at `<data_dir>/reports/baselines/`.
These are generic defaults when no baseline exists.

## Body Composition
- Body fat: < 15% for males, < 22% for females
- Lean mass: trending up
- Weight: secondary metric, monitor only

## Cardiovascular
- ApoB: < 60 mg/dL (gold standard for ASCVD risk)
- LDL-C: < 100 mg/dL (use until ApoB available)
- BP: < 120/80
- Resting HR: trending down
- HRV: trending up

## Metabolic
- CGM avg glucose: < 100 mg/dL
- Glucose SD: < 15 mg/dL
- Time in range (70-110): > 90%
- A1c: < 5.3%

## Sleep
- Total: 7-8.5 hrs
- Deep: > 15% of total
- REM: > 20% of total

## Fitness
- Zone 2: ≥ 3 hrs/week (see **coach-cardio** skill)
- Strength: 3-4 sessions/week (see **coach-strength** skill)
- Protein: 1.6-2.2 g/kg/day (see **coach-nutrition** skill)
